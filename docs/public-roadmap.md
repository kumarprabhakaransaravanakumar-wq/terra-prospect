# Public Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Closed-loop contract

- Separate hidden truth, onboard belief, and independent evaluation.
- Establish probabilistic concentration and depth mapping.
- Compare utility-aware sensing with deterministic coverage baselines.
- Confirm targets with a bounded drill-calibration loop.

## Phase 2 - Sensor and spatial fidelity

- Expand modality-specific uncertainty contracts.
- Add correlated spatial priors and pose uncertainty.
- Validate synthetic observations against suitable public analogue data.
- Stress out-of-distribution and conflicting-sensor behavior.

## Phase 3 - Vehicle and mission constraints

- Couple planning to mobility, power, thermal, and drill constraints.
- Add deterministic safety monitoring and fault injection.
- Run randomized campaigns across multiple environment classes.

## Phase 4 - Hardware and field validation

- Measure execution timing on representative edge compute.
- Integrate hardware-in-the-loop instrument and actuator interfaces.
- Conduct independently scored analogue prospecting trials.

Only disclosure-safe milestones and results will be mirrored into this public repository.
